## About Randall

Randall's a native of Miamisburg, Ohio, who's spent decades building a career in music performance — degrees from Florida State (D.M.), the University of Florida (M.M.), and Wright State (B.M.), with time at the College-Conservatory of Music at Cincinnati along the way.
