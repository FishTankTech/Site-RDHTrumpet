About Randall

A native of Ohio, now teaching in Tennessee.

Dr. Randall Haynes is a freelance performer and instructor based in Franklin, TN, originally from Miamisburg, Ohio. He holds a D.M. from The Florida State University, an M.M. from the University of Florida, and a B.M. from Wright State University, with additional study at the College-Conservatory of Music at the University of Cincinnati.

His primary teachers include Bryan Goff, Christopher Moore, Joyce Davis, Karl Sievers, William Adam, Alan Siebert, and David Coleman. He currently substitutes with the Hendersonville Symphony Orchestra (NC), and is available for private lessons, solo performance, masterclasses, and ensemble work.
